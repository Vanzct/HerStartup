On July 20, 2016, TechBase, SoGal， Lean In China and Krspace, are proud to jointly host the Her Startup Competition to showcase the creativity and strength of female entrepreneurs. Outstanding startups will be selected to receive support through funding, cross-border education resources, and access to further business development opportunities in order to promote the attainment of entrepreneurial success.

If you are selected, you will be invited to participate in an entire week of events relating to Her Startup in US commencing July 18 to July 22 including an intensive pre-competition bootcamp and a tour of major Silicon Valley tech companies with the final competition held on July 20, 2016.Apart from our authority experts, our bootcamp will create for participants’ authentic experience of the entrepreneurial culture of Silicon Valley, of which our educational Partners include Stanford Graduate School of Business, Draper University, Y Combinator and 500 Startups.
